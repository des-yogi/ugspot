What we offer

No- obligation visit, no-obligation advice - Ultimate Solution

At UGS Ltd, we make the process of upgrading your property simple and convenient with our free, no-obligation surveying appointment. Our head surveyor will visit your property to provide a comprehensive consultation, ensuring that every detail is covered.

During the visit, our head surveyor will:

  1. Discuss your specific requirements to fully understand your needs and preferences.
  2. Take precise measurements of units to ensure a perfect fit.
  3. Assess the property and access, so we can plan the installation efficiently.
  4. Offer professional advice, guiding you on the best solutions for insulation, noise reduction, or security based on your property.

Following the appointment, UGS Ltd will provide you with an exact quote, tailored to the specifications discussed. With a full understanding of your project, we will recommend the optimal and most cost-effective solution, ensuring the best value for your investment.
