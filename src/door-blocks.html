Block Headline

At UGS Ltd, we believe that every timber door should not only meet functional requirements but also reflect your unique style and preferences. That’s why all our timber door products—whether standard or bespoke—come with extensive personalisation options to suit your needs. From entrance doors to bi-fold and sliding patio doors, our customisation features ensure your door perfectly complements your property while offering exceptional performance.

Door Hanging Options

Every door type we offer can be tailored with various hanging options to suit your installation preferences and space requirements. Whether you need a left or right-hinged door or a specific folding or sliding mechanism, we ensure each unit operates seamlessly and fits perfectly within your architectural framework.

Locking Mechanisms

Security is paramount, and our timber doors come with UKAS-approved locking mechanisms as standard, providing peace of mind. For enhanced security, we offer additional features such as multi-point locking systems and anti-snap, anti-drill, and anti-bump locks. For those seeking modern convenience, our range includes smart locks that enable keyless entry and remote control via smartphone apps, combining security with cutting-edge technology.

Unit Accessories

Each timber door can be further personalized with a wide variety of accessories. From handles, hinges, and letterplates to thresholds and weather seals, every detail can be tailored to your preferences. These accessories are carefully selected to complement the style and functionality of each door type, ensuring a cohesive and refined finish.

At UGS Ltd, personalisation is at the heart of what we do. Whether you’re selecting a standard unit or designing a bespoke piece, we offer unmatched flexibility to ensure your timber door is a perfect fit for your home and lifestyle.

Colour Options Inside and Out

UGS Ltd offers an extensive palette of colour options, allowing you to personalize the interior and exterior finishes of your timber doors. Choose a single colour for uniformity or opt for dual-colour finishes to differentiate the door’s appearance on each side. From classic wood stains that enhance the natural grain to bold, modern colours, we provide endless possibilities to match your style and décor.

Glazing Options

Our glazing options cater to both aesthetics and performance. Choose from double or triple glazing units with or without Argon gas for superior insulation and energy efficiency. For added customization, we provide Pilkington glazing designs, allowing you to select decorative patterns and textures that enhance the visual appeal of your timber doors while maintaining privacy and light transmission.

Sash Windows (Vertical Sliders) – Glazing Options for Enhanced Comfort

At UGS Ltd, our sash windows (vertical sliders) are available with both double and triple glazing options to provide superior energy efficiency and comfort for your home. Whether you choose double or triple glazing, both offer excellent thermal insulation and noise reduction, making your home more comfortable and energy-efficient year-round. For enhanced performance, our glazing units can be filled with argon gas, an inert gas that increases thermal efficiency by reducing heat transfer through the glass. Argon-filled windows also help to reduce condensation buildup and improve overall energy savings, ensuring a warm, comfortable environment while lowering energy bills. For character properties or listed buildings where replacing the original windows is not possible, secondary glazing units are the ideal solution. They preserve the exterior charm while significantly improving insulation and noise reduction. To learn more, visit our Secondary Glazing Unit page for more details on how this can be the perfect upgrade.
