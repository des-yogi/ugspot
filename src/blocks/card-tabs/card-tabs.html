Types of secondary glazing units:

UGS Ltd offers a comprehensive range of secondary glazing units, available in both standard and made-to-measure custom sizes to suit any project. We supply and install secondary glazing in aluminium with options available in both Standard and Heritage (slim) profiles. Choose from a variety of finishes, including the full RAL colour spectrum, and select from single, double or triple glazing to meet your specific needs and budget.
