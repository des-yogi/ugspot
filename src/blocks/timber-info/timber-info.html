Softwood vs. Hardwood: Key Comparisons

Softwood is generally more budget-friendly and easier to customize, making it a practical choice for interior settings or decorative purposes. However, it may require regular maintenance and treatment to ensure long-term durability. Hardwood, on the other hand, is more robust and naturally resistant to damage, offering superior performance for high-traffic areas and external use.

While hardwood typically comes at a higher initial cost, its durability and minimal maintenance needs often make it a cost-effective investment in the long run.
