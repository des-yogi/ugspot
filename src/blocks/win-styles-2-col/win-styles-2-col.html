Standard vs. Slim Profiles – Finding the Perfect Window Solution

When choosing windows, the profile type plays a crucial role in both aesthetics and performance. At UGS Ltd, we offer a wide selection of standard and slim profiles, allowing you to find the perfect balance between design, functionality, and energy efficiency for your project.

Standart

Offer a robust and traditional frame appearance, providing excellent insulation, durability, and support for larger glass panes. These profiles are ideal for those who prioritize structural integrity and maximum thermal performance. These frames are typically more common and versatile, allowing for larger glass panes and providing excellent insulation. The wider frame also supports more customization options in terms of materials and finishes. While they offer great structural support, they can sometimes reduce the amount of visible glass, which slightly affects the natural light entering your space.

Slim

On the other hand, slim profiles are designed with sleek, narrow frames to maximize glass area and allow more natural light into your space. Perfect for modern designs or properties where a minimalist look is desired, slim profiles provide a refined aesthetic while still maintaining energy efficiency and strength.
